10 Ponder : Spiritual Learning Challenge - Sacrifice

Refer to 3 Nephi 9:15-20. Elder Neal A. Maxwell of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles said: "Real, personal sacrifice never was placing an animal on the altar. Instead, it is a willingness to put the animal in us upon the altar and letting it be consumed!" ("Deny Yourselves of All Ungodliness," Ensign, May 1995, 68).

Your challenge this week is to find a chance to identify something that you can give up or sacrifice to come to know the Savior better and then commit to do so.

If you feel uncomfortable with this challenge for any reason, you are welcome to determine an alternative that follows in the same spirit, but is more appropriate for your individual circumstances.

If you accept this challenge, please prayerfully consider how you might accomplish it this week, share your progress during class, and finally give a short report of how it went.
