03 Teach : Team Project - Team Formation

This week, you need to form teams for your semester data mining projects. These are teams of three (two if necessary, rather than four). Please refer to the Team Project Requirements for more information about the scope and requirements of the project.

Then, based on some of the brainstorming ideas you shared in the first week, please use the dicussion board from this week to help self-organize.

By the end of the week, please submit a plain text document with the following information:

1. List the members of the proposed team.

2. Briefly describe your high-level idea for a project.

Please note that the instructor reserves the right to assign team members / change teams, especially in the case that someone fails to self-identify or there are inconsistencies regarding proposed members.

Also, note that your project idea can still change after this point, but you should have something in mind to start from.
